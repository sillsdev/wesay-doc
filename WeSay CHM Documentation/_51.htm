Email This Dictionary

You can use this tool to email your entire dictionary to someone else, provided you have a working email address. Click Set Up to enter the recipient's email address, and then you will have the option to make the button visible in WeSay. Once the button is in WeSay, you can click it to send your dictionary. WeSay will ask you for your name, email address and then information about your email server. After you enter this information, you will be able to send your dictionary through email.
